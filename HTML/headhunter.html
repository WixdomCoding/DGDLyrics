Head Hunter

Produced by Kris Crummett

About the Song:

“Head Hunter” is Dance Gavin Dance’s first single since the release of Artificial Selection in 2018. The track was released alongside a music video directed by Samuel Halleen, who worked on the band’s popular hits like “Midnight Crusade”, “Betrayed by the Game” and “Inspire The Liars”.

The lyrics seem to describe Jon Mess and his ascension to a godlike status, and Tillian seems to be making an attempt to swoon a lady

This is the first installment of what would become a duology with the track “Blood Wolf”, since both the artworks can be put together in order to complete a picture.
