The Ghost of Billy Royalton

Produced by Kris Crummett, Stephen Hawkes & Zach Jones

About the Song:

“The Ghost of Billy Royalton” serves as an ode to Dance Gavin Dance’s long-time bassist, Tim Feerick, who unfortunately passed away on April 13, 2022.

Feerick started out as a touring bassist for the band in 2009, but officially became a member in November 2012 and appeared on all releases from 2013 to 2022.

Tim was dear to both the band and fans, who reunited in the Afterburner mural in Sacramento and lit up candles in his honor while playing DGD songs. Tilian joined one of the fans' live streams and stayed grieving with fans.

This song serves as a memory to Tim and contains explicit messages from the band members to him. Most notably, the outro from Andrew Wells captures his grief while band veterans Jon Mess and Will Swan keep the spirit of Dance Gavin Dance alive.
