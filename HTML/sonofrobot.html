About the Song:

“Son of Robot” is the second single off of Dance Gavin Dance’s 2018 album, Artificial Selection, and the most recent continuation of the band’s ‘Robot With Human Hair’ series.

On Nov. 6, an animated music video for “Son of Robot” was released, animated by John Howe.
