Prisoner

Produced by Drew Fulk & Kris Crummett

Featuring Sergio Medina

About the Song:

“Prisoner” describes a hallucinogenic trip and the thoughts associated with that drug experience. This idea is backed by the psychodelic visuals of the music video directed by Orie McGinness.

This is the lead single and opening track of Dance Gavin Dance’s ninth full-length album Afterburner. The song features guitarist Sergio Medina, from Stolas, Sianvar, Royal Coda, and Nova Charisma.
